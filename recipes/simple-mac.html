Simple Mac

With simle mac anything can be paired withs! Or it can be eaten completely by itself. Scroll down to the botttom how to make this simple recipe in minutes.

Ingridients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box of macaroni elbows
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • ground pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil Macaroni
  2. Make a Roux
  3. Add cheese and Macaroni

Now this cheesy mac is ready for consumption! Enjoy!
